Locigraph begins from a dissatisfaction with note systems that preserve where a thought was stored while losing what the thought later became.

Its working model separates observations, claims, concepts, revisions, contradictions, and tasks. The objective is not merely storage. It is to preserve the movement of understanding over time and make that movement navigable.

## Current phase

Phase IV focuses on the planetary retrieval layer and the systems required to move between concepts rather than files.

The current state should not be mistaken for production readiness. The core architecture is being built, while authentication, deployment, operational hardening, and reliable public access remain later work.

## Intended shape

- concept-centric retrieval
- explicit claim revision
- contradiction tracking
- method-of-loci spatial navigation
- custodial and librarian agents with reviewable authority
- private-first deployment
