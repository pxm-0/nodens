The current chain is simple: Apple Music lossless into the Topping DX3 Pro+, then the Mega5EST.

The interesting part is not the list.

A chain becomes correct when I stop listening to the chain. Placement becomes obvious without calling attention to separation. Texture arrives without the treble filing a report. The equipment does its work and then vacates the frame.

That disappearance is the standard.
