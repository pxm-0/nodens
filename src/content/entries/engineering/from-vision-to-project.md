Most personal projects do not fail at the idea. They disappear in the distance between architecture and operation.

Oreo Cloud is an attempt to make that distance ordinary.

The goal is a deployment surface I understand end to end: container build, routing, secrets, storage, logs, health, rollback, and the physical machine underneath it all.

Not a toy AWS dashboard. A small cloud with no place to hide its machinery.
