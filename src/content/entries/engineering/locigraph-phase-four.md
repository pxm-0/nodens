Locigraph is in **Phase IV of VI**.

That statement means the core architecture has crossed a meaningful threshold. It does not mean the application is ready for strangers, traffic, or the thousand small failures that arrive after deployment.

## What exists

The current system has an increasingly coherent model for observations, claims, concepts, revisions, contradictions, and spatial retrieval.

## What does not

Authentication is not complete. Deployment has not been hardened. Operational readiness is not something I am willing to infer from a successful local build.

A project can be conceptually mature and operationally young at the same time. Locigraph currently occupies that exact territory.
