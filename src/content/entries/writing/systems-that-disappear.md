The best systems do not demand continuous recognition.

A pen disappears from the hand. A deployment path disappears behind one reliable command. A relationship to an object becomes so exact that the object stops interrupting the act it serves.

This is not minimalism. Minimalism is visible. It announces the absence.

Correctness is quieter.
