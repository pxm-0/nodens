**Dose:** 14.6 g  
**Method:** V60  
**Grind:** medium  
**Total time:** approximately 3:30

The first cup was not bad. It was worse than bad in a more useful way: boring.

The acidity was mild. The structure was clean enough. Nothing arrived with enough force to justify the ritual.

The second brew moved louder and more bitter, which at least proved the coffee had somewhere to go. The target is now clearer: more presence without sacrificing clarity, and enough sweetness that sugar becomes optional rather than corrective.
